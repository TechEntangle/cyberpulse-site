About

CyberPulse is built for decision-makers who need signal fast.

CyberPulse is a modern executive cyber intelligence publication focused on the overlap between cyber risk, artificial intelligence, infrastructure exposure, identity abuse, and board-level decision making.

It is designed to feel more like a premium briefing product than a conventional newsletter. Web-first design, daily archival publishing, automated cover generation, and a reading experience closer to a product than a blog.

What it covers

Cyber operations, exploited vulnerabilities, leadership-targeted attacks, cloud and identity risk, regulatory shifts, AI threat acceleration, and the strategic implications behind fast-moving headlines.

How it is written

Each briefing is structured to answer what happened, why it matters, and what smart operators, CISOs, boards, and risk committees should do next.

Signal over noise Every edition strips away the clutter. No link dumps, no filler. Board-ready analysis of the developments that actually change risk posture.
Web-native publishing Direct-to-domain on tusharvartak.com. No platform lock-in, no walled gardens. Fast, mobile-first, and designed for social sharing.
Independent perspective Not vendor-sponsored, not algorithm-optimized. Written by Tushar Vartak with the independence that executive-grade intelligence requires.
